Bio

Lucas is a self-taught 20-year-old designer from Curitiba, Brazil currently working in Boston, MA. He specializes in User Interface design with a focus on clean minimalist visuals and user-centered information architecture. Always looking at the big picture, his background in game design and photography allow him to solve real world problems in an unorthodox manner.

When Lucas isn't learning something new or referring to himself in the third person, he can be found:

  • Taking photos of strangers
  • Coaching Parkour for Motion Revolution
  • Tutoring Startup Institute Students in Front End Design
  • Answering Branding and UX Questions on Reddit's /r/designcritiques
  • Developing the 238th Most Popular Moddb Mod
