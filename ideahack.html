Jess, Meet Ken

As a student at Startup Institute participating in IdeaHack, I was assigned a 5 person team of classmates and given 24 hours to solve a user retention problem with the early stage dating startup Jess, Meet Ken. We were given 4 problems to solve, one of which was mandatory, the rest being optional given the limited time. We were told to:

  1. Solve the issue of women not communicating inside the site.
  2. Figure out how to retain users after they find a date.
  3. Come up with a way to attract male users to a female dominated site.
  4. Figure out how to monetize the system without ad revenue.

We solved all four problems by designing and defining the user flow of a new mobile app for the company (Axure demo). The powerpoint deck for our winning pitch can also be seen here.
